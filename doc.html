More info on my blog http://jpascal.wordpress.com

Shortcuts:

Press SPACEBAR to show/hide weather data
Press ENTER to pause/star the animation
Press 'v' to see the vector map
Press 'd' to show/hide debug information
Press 'a' to auto adjust particle number.
Press UP ARROW to add particles
Press DOWN ARROW to remove particles
Press 1-6 to use fake data scenario
